# Mt. Battle Sources

- Altissimo supplies the calculator's trainer identities, fixed abilities and natures, moves, items, levels, Battle Mode IV/EV spreads, and Story/XD listed stats.
- Bulbapedia's Mt. Battle Area 1-10 pages verify every battle's trainer identity, roster, fixed level, and each area leader's detailed sets where available.
- Story Mode and XD IV/EV values are reconstructed as legal spreads that reproduce Altissimo's listed final stats exactly. Shedinja uses its in-battle 1 HP rather than Altissimo's displayed pre-ability HP calculation.
- Colosseum Battle Mode opponents match the player's highest-level party member, with a minimum level of 50.
- Every battle number maps to exactly one trainer in each mode.

The generator records these source conflicts explicitly and fails on any unknown mismatch:

- Colosseum Story battle 30: Altissimo includes Sweet Scent on Surskit; Bulbapedia omits it.
- Colosseum Story battle 64: Altissimo lists Ninjask at level 46; Bulbapedia lists level 48.
- Colosseum Story battle 80: Altissimo lists Hariyama with Guts; Bulbapedia lists Thick Fat.
- Colosseum Story battle 82: Altissimo lists Camerupt at level 56; Bulbapedia lists level 55.
- Colosseum Battle Mode Singles battle 50: Altissimo lists Mirror Move on Murkrow; Bulbapedia lists Mirror Coat.
- XD battle 9: Altissimo lists Baltoy at level 11; Bulbapedia lists level 12.
- XD battle 34: Altissimo lists Corsola at level 30; Bulbapedia lists level 29.
- XD battle 53: Altissimo lists Vibrava at level 44; Bulbapedia lists level 43.
- XD battle 70: Altissimo names the Area Leader Nocon; Bulbapedia names him Nocom.
- XD battle 80: Altissimo lists Rock Tomb on Machamp; Bulbapedia lists Rock Slide.
